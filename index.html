OVERVIEW
According to the 17 Sustainable Development Goals, good health and well-being are one of them. There are many related applications in the market right now. However, one thing the application mostly does not give is motivation so some of the users who use health applications or people who set their goals cannot follow the plan. The study from the University of Scranton states that a whopping 92% of people who set New Year's goals actually never achieve them[1]. Therefore, it is important to give them the motivation to be that  8%. Moreover, Healthdirect, the free Australian health adviser said that motivation is important because it provides users with goals to work towards, helps them change old habits and helps them cope with challenges and opportunities[2].
